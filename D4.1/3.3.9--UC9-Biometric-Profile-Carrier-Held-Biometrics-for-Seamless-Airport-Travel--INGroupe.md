### UC 9: Biometric Profile - Carrier Held Biometrics - for Seamless Airport Travel (IN Groupe)
To develop our UC, we worked on compliance this the ARF, the eIDAS Regulation and its Implementing Acts, as well as the French Wallet Technical Rules and Specifications derived from eIDAS. 
We have been working collaboratively using shared documents, Figma files, Mermaid flows, and folders within a common workspace, with the goal of transferring all content to NextCloud for the final deliverable.
