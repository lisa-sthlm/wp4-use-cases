#### Trusted Issuer Lists
UC9 respects the governance of the eIDAS 2.0 Regulation and the technical specifications defined by the Architecture and Reference Framework. The trust model is built upon a cross-border interoperability layer where the EUDI Wallets provided France Identité for France and the Credential like PID or DTC issued by entities within the European digital identity ecosystem. This framework ensures that credentials issued in one Member State is recognized as authentic and legally valid by Relying Parties in another, maintaining a high level of trustworthiness for all exchanged credentials. Credentials will be shared using the OPENID4VC standards to ensure interoperability full compliance with the EUDIW trust framework.
