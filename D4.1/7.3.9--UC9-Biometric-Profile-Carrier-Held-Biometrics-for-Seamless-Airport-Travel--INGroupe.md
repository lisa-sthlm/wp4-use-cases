### UC 9: Biometric Profile - Carrier Held Biometrics - for Seamless Airport Travel (IN Groupe)
UC9 use case pilots the issuance by IN Groupe and verification of a Biometric Profile attestation allowing a traveller to seamlessly go through each of the Marseille airport touchpoints from luggage drop to boarding in full compliance with OpenID4VC/VP standards.

Given the scope of this UC, the issuance of the Digital Travel Credential is considered a precondition; the testing scenario assumes that traveller have already provisioned their credentials within their EUDIW. Similarly, while revocation is not the primary focus of the UC, it is an implied requirement to ensure the ongoing validity of the credentials.
