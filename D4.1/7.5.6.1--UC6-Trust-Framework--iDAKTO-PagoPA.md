#### Trust Framework

UC6 operates within the governance of the **eIDAS 2.0 Regulation** and adheres to the technical specifications defined by the **Architecture and Reference Framework.** 
The trust model is built upon a cross-border interoperability layer where the EUDI Wallets (provided by PagoPA for Italy and France Identité for France) and the Credential Issuers (such as Polimi and GYSC) are certified entities within the European digital identity ecosystem. This framework ensures that the digital Student Card issued in one Member State is recognized as authentic and legally valid by Relying Parties (e.g., Airweb/Irigo or the University of Angers) in another, maintaining a high level of trustwotrhiness for all exchanged credentials.

Interoperability is granted by the adoption of **OPENID4VC** by all partners of this scenario, that enables the compliance with the EUDIW trust framework.
