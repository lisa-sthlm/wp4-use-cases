### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey (PagoPA)

#### Sources and Tools

The development of UC7 relies on the combination of the entire EUDIW documentation: 
- **ARF**, as the primary baseline for the the role definition of stakeholders and the entire EUDIW infrastructure, in the actual Trenitalia processes;
- **OpenID4VCI and OpenID4VP protocol specifications**, as the essential principles for the Wallet interactions;
- **eIDAS 2.0 Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2024/1183** and its Implementing Acts, useful to define technical requirements and the core functioning of the entire Wallet ecosystem;
- **Italian Wallet Technical Specifications** based on eIDAS, https://italia.github.io/eid-wallet-it-docs/versione-corrente/en/introduction.html, which are the Italian Reference for the Digital Wallet Infrastructure.

As far as **working tools**, UC7 has been developed via an internal shared repository for documents, deliverables and internal evaluations. Figma files and folders on Google Workspace have been the main tools, so as to transfer the content on NextCloud for the deliverable.
