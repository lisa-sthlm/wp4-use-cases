#### Issuance

The primary credentials for UC7 are the PID and the European Disability Card, both issued by the official Italian Public Issuer, **Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato** (IPZS).
As detailed in previous chapters, the actual issuance process is **out of scope** for this scenario. UC7 operates on the precondition that the passenger has already:
- Obtained and activated an EUDI Wallet (provided by PagoPA);
- Completed the issuance process for their Disability Card.

Although the issuance phase lies outside the scenario boundaries, IPZS issues these credentials in compliance with the **OpenID4VCI standard**. This ensures the trustworthiness, integrity, and validity of the verifiable credentials at the time of verification by the Relying Party.
