#### Revocation

As the issuance, Revocation process is not core for UC6, but it is limited to the verification of the credential status during the presentation process. In this case, the validity of the credential is synchronized with the **student's enrollment status**.
Revocation management relies on eIDAS 2.0 standards (such as Status Lists), ensuring that if a student graduates or loses their device, the ESC credential can be invalidated across the ecosystem.
