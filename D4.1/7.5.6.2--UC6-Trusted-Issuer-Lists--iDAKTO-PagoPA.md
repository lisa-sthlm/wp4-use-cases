Within UC6, the Relying Parties, such as Airweb and the University of Angers, are able to validate Student credentials since the Issuers are recognized as  partners of **APTITUDE Pilot’s Trusted Issuer List**. This mechanism enables a programmatic verification process, ensuring that the European Student Card and the associated status attestations are generated exclusively by authorized entities, namely Politecnico di Milano (Polimi) and GYSC.

The reliability and security of these digital certificates can be granted since:
- each attestation is **authenticated through the use of public keys and digital signatures**, which provide the proof of integrity from the moment of issuance.
- as **official academic and administrative institutions**, Polimi and GYSC serve as the sole competent authorities for releasing identity attributes that Relying Parties can process with an absolute degree of certainty.

To operate within a protected testing environment, the pilot will utilize mock data, thereby avoiding any interference with actual production systems. Nevertheless, the verification logic, the underlying protocols (OpenID4VCI/VP), and the entire operational flow strictly mirror the eIDAS 2.0 ecosystem.
