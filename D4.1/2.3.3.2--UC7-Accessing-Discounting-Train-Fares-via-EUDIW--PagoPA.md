#### UC 7: Accessing Discounting Train Fares via EUDIW (PagoPA)

UC7 and UC8 enable passengers to book train travel through the Trenitalia app by leveraging the EUDI Wallet provided by PagoPA. In both scenarios, the workflow is initiated by a primary interaction between the Wallet and the Relying Party system, where passengers share specific credentials to personalize their booking:
- **UC7 (Disability Discounts)**: passengers share their PID and European Disability Card to apply for discounted fares and, where applicable, a complimentary ticket for a companion.
- **UC8 (Identity and Cabin Reservation)**: passengers present their identity credentials (PID and/or DTC) to verify their gender, enabling the reservation of women-only cabins for overnight journeys.

This verification phase is a mandatory step that occurs prior to payment, ensuring that the booking is finalized based on verified eligibility.
Furthermore, given the involvement of **PagoPA and Trenitalia in WP6 Payment Scenario**, there is the potential to extend these use cases to include payment authorization. This would utilize Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) issued by Bancomat, creating a seamless end-to-end experience from identity verification to transaction finalization. Such evolutions will be further evaluated during the development of the pilot within the WP6 framework.
