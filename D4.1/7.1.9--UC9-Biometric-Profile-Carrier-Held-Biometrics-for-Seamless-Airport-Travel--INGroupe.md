### UC 9: Biometric Profile - Carrier Held Biometrics - for Seamless Airport Travel (IN Groupe)
UC9 The Biometric Profile for seamless airport travel use case implementation aligns with the EUDIW ARF by leveraging its core roles and components:

* The wallet instance is used by travellers to identify himself. It requests the traveller’s authorization to share attributes from the following attestation PID and/or PhotoID and/or DTC to the Marseille Airport app. L’ANTS will provide the EUDI Wallet.
* The PID provider ensures the initial binding of the wallet to a verified identity, enabling trust in the credential holder.
* The attestation provider, IN Groupe is responsible for issuing the Biometric Profile attestation to the Marseille Airport app.
* Finally, the relying party, Marseille airports and its main touchpoints request and verify the credential to grant access to services or benefits.

The implementation follows the protocol profiles defined in the ARF for credential issuance and presentation, ensuring secure, interoperable exchanges between wallet, issuers, and relying parties. This could include support for selective disclosure, allowing travellers to prove their status without sharing unnecessary personal data.

The baseline version used is aligned with the latest available ARF specifications applicable to the project, ensuring consistency with ongoing developments under eIDAS 2.0 and compatibility with other use cases within WP4.
