### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO-PagoPA)

UC6 primarily pilots the **Verification** of student status for French and Italian students to facilitate access to specific services. At its core, the use case focuses on the verification of the Student Card by the University of Angers and local transportation providers (e.g., Airweb/Irigo), in full compliance with **OpenID4VP standards**.

Given the scope of this UC, the **issuance** of the Student Card is considered a precondition; the testing scenario assumes that students have already provisioned their credentials within their EUDIW. Similarly, while **revocation** is not the primary focus of the UC, it is an implied requirement to ensure the ongoing validity of the credentials.
