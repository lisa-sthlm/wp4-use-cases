### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO-PagoPA)

UC6 fully relies on **User Consent and Selective Disclosure** so as to preserve student privacy.
User Consent is central to the cross-border verification process. Through the EUDI Wallet, students maintain full control of their personal data, explicitly authorizing the sharing of their academic status RPs. 
By leveraging Selective Disclosure, students can prove their eligibility for university services or transportation discounts by sharing only the necessary status confirmation, without disclosing unrelated personal information contained in their PID or Student Card.
