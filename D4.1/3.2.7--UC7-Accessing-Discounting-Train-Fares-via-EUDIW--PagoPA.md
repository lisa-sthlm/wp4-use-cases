### UC 7: Accessing Discounting Train Fares via EUDIW (PagoPA)

**Stakeholder Involvement**

The definition and development of UC7 has been possibile thanks to the interactions between the two main stakeholders involved: PagoPA and Trenitalia, who have worked together to identify key points of the actual process and the possibile evolutions thorugh the implementation of EUDIW. More specifically:
- **PagoPA**is acting as Wallet Provider and lead for this UC. 
- **Trenitalia** as a train service provider acting as Relying Party for the verification of the Disability Card, or other credentials, in order to personalize passengers' tickets on their app.
- **Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato** acting as the italian official issuer for PID and Disability Card.

Starting from the definition of the use case, stalkeholders have cooperated on:
- **Bilateral meetings**, useful to explore actual processes and define possibile interactions, by clarifying any possible doubts;
- **Shared collaborative documents**, used to work directly on the final deliverables before trasnferring them to NextCloud;

The final aim was to find an alternative solution to the actual processes in which integrating the Wallet infrastructure, providing users with the full control of their identity.
