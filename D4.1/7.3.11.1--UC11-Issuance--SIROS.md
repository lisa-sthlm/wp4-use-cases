#### Issuance

- PID issuance follows the French national framework under ANTS, into the student's EUDIW prior to travel.

- Student EAA issuance is performed by GYSC into the same EUDIW, using OpenID4VCI or the equivalent ARF-profiled flow. [TBC: exact profile.]

- Student-diploma credential issuance, if included later, follows the issuer's own ARF-profiled issuance flow.
