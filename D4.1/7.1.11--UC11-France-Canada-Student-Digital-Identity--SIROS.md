### UC 11: France-Canada Student Digital Identity (SIROS)

UC 11 uses the following ARF roles:

**Wallet instance**: an EU-issued EUDIW under the French framework, held on the student's device.

**PID provider**: the French government, operationally via ANTS.

**Attestation (EAA) provider**: GYSC, issuer of the French student card. A French student-diploma credential may be added in a later iteration.

**Relying parties**: Canadian universities, student-housing providers, and (possibly) transit operators and Canadian government services. Open-source verifier components may be provided by the consortium where native support is missing.
