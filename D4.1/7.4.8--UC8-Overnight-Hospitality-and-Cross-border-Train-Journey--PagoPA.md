### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey (PagoPA)

Considering the specific type of train journey, **UC8 relies on Selective Disclosure, User Consent and Data minimization** so as to streamline passenger experience, in compliance with privacy-preserving conditions. 
User Consent ensures that passengers are aware of and authorize every data exchange with train operators. Selective Disclosure allows travelers to share only specific attributes required for that trip. This ensures that sensitive identity data remains protected while enabling a seamless, secure transit experience across borders.
