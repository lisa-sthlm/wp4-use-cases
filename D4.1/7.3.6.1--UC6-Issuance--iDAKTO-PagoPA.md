#### Issuance

An **Italian student** obtains their student card issued by **Polimi** directly on their EUDIW, provided by **PagoPA**. On the French side, a French student obtains their student card issued by **GYSC** on their EUDIW, provided by **ANTS**.

Although the  issuance follows the **OpenID4VCI protocol**, ensuring a secure link between the university's student registry and the mobile wallet, the Use case implies that the students have already obtained their student card. The Use Case core regards the presentation of the credential for accessing services.
