### UC 7: Accessing Discounting Train Fares via EUDIW (PagoPA)

UC7 primary objective is to enhance the booking experience for passengers with specific needs, leveraging the principle of data minimization. 
When interacting with the Trenitalia platform, passengers must provide explicit **User Consent** before any data is transmitted from their EUDI Wallet, especially regarding their disability. The use of **Selective Disclosure** allows the system to verify specific eligibility criteria, such as the "companion entitlement" or disability status, ensuring that only the minimum required attributes are shared to apply the correct fare while keeping the rest of the user’s identity private.
