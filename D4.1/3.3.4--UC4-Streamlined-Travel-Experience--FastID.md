### UC 4: Streamlined Travel Experience (FastID)

## Sources

### EUDI Wallet Architecture and Reference Framework
Defines the overall wallet architecture and roles used in this use case, including wallet, PID Provider, Attestation Provider and Relying Party interactions. UC 4.4 follows the ARF baseline for credential issuance and presentation flows.  
https://eudi.dev/2.8.0/#architecture-and-reference-framework

### eIDAS 2.0 and Implementing Acts
Provides the legal and technical foundation for the European Digital Identity Wallet ecosystem, including requirements for wallet functionality, trust and interoperability.  
https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=OJ:L_202401183

### OpenID4VCI and OpenID4VP
Protocol specs used for credential issuance and presentation between wallet and relying party.

### ICAO Digital Travel Credential standards
Basis for deriving travel credentials from passport data and ensuring compatibility with international travel document standards.
https://www.icao.int/sites/default/files/TRIP/Publications/High-Level-Guidance-explaining-ICAO-DTC.pdf
