#### Selective Disclosure

UC 11 applies data minimisation across the flow: at each presentation only the attributes needed by the specific relying party are disclosed. For example, nationality only for a French-nationality check; student-status plus institution for a student-housing eligibility check. The exact attribute set per presentation will be fixed in the detailed design. Credential-format choice (see 7.2.11) determines the selective-disclosure mechanism available — for example, SD-JWT salted-hash disclosures or mdoc issuer-signed selective disclosure.
