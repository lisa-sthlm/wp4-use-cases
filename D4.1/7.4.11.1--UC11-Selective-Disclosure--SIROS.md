#### Selective Disclosure

UC 11 operates across two distinct trust frameworks: the EU eIDAS / ARF trust framework on the issuing side, and the DIACC Pan-Canadian Trust Framework (PCTF) as the de-facto reference framework on the Canadian relying-party side. A bridging arrangement for the pilot is built on the ANTS–Quebec cooperation agreement and the SIROS–DIACC arrangement connected to it. Formalising how a Canadian relying party can legitimately trust an EU-issued credential — and vice versa — is itself a subject of UC 11 and will be documented as a finding of the pilot.
