#### Verification

Verification is performed by the Canadian relying party via its verifier component, using the EUDIW-profiled presentation protocol (OpenID4VP). Where a Canadian relying party does not have native verifier support, the consortium will provide an open-source verifier.


The verifier evaluates the issuer's trust status (see 7.5.11), validates the credential signature and freshness, and checks the selective-disclosure proofs for the disclosed attributes.
