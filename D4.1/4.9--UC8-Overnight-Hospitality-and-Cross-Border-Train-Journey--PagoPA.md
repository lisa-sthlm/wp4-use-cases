## UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-Border Train Journey (PagoPA)
UC8 aims to streamline cross-border travel with Trenitalia by enabling passengers to use their EUDI Wallet (via PagoPA) for both online and physical interactions. Passengers can seamlessly book an overnight journey from Rome to Monaco (Eurocity Notte trains) and reserve dedicated cabins (e.g. women-only cabins), by sharing their PID or DTC remotely during the purchase phase, and subsequently verifying their identity in-person (proximity mode) while on board the train.
Nowadays the system requires the manual verification of physical documents and the introduction of the EUDIW framework would simplify the entire flow, for all European passengers.
