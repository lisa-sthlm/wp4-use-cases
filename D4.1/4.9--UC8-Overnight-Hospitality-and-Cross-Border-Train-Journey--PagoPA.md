## UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-Border Train Journey (PagoPA)

### Use Case overview

**UC8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-Border Train Journeys** aims to streamline cross-border travel with Trenitalia by enabling passengers to use their EUDI Wallet (via PagoPA) for both online and physical interactions. Passengers can seamlessly book an overnight journey from Rome to Monaco (Eurocity Notte trains) and reserve dedicated cabins (e.g. women-only cabins), by sharing their PID or DTC remotely during the purchase phase, and subsequently verifying their identity in-person (proximity mode) while on board the train.

This UC contributes to WP4 objectives by addressing the **Scenario 1: Streamlined Travel Experience** and partially **Scenario 2: Hotel Check-in**, since it shows how EUDIW can overcome actual manual verification systems in order to simplify the entire flow, for all European passengers. In this case, passengers from all over Europe can book an overnight train ticket on Trenitalia app, and they can reserve a cabin directly by checking their identity via PagoPA EUDIW. 

Nowadays, these journeys require manual identity verification, both on Trenitalia app and on the train, asking passengers to use their physical ID to demonstrate theit right to a women-only shared cabin. The integration with EUDIW would allow passengers to share their PID or any other identity credential, such as DTC, with Trenitalia, both online and in proximity on the train.
