#### Revocation
Revocation process is not core for UC9 and can impact the verification of the credential status during the presentation process. The validity of the credential is verified using the travellers DTC status for example. Revocation management relies on eIDAS 2.0 standards (such as Status Lists), ensuring that if a traveller credential expires or loses their device, traveller biometric profile or ready to travel attestation can’t be issued or is invalid.
