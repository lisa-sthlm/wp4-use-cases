### UC 9: Biometric Profile - Carrier Held Biometrics - for Seamless Airport Travel (IN Groupe)

The definition and development of UC9 have been carried out by two main stakeholders involved: IN Groupe and Marseille Airport, who have worked together to identify key points of the actual process and the possible evolutions through the implementation of EUDIW. More specifically:

  * IN Groupe, lead to this UC, as the biometric profile attestation and Ready to travel Attestation issuer
  * Marseille Airport, the airport where the experiment will take place acting as Relying Party for the verification of the biometric profile and Ready to travel Attestation issuer
  * France Titres — Agence Nationale des Titres Sécurisés (ANTS) as the EUDI Wallet provider

 Starting from the definition of the use case, stakeholders have cooperated on:
 
  * Bilateral meetings, useful to explore actual processes and define possible interactions, by clarifying any possible doubts;

   * Shared collaborative documents, used to work directly on the final deliverables before transferring them to NextCloud;

Our goal is to find improve our current biometric profile solution by integrating the Wallet infrastructure, providing users a better user experience, more security, and with the full control of their identity
